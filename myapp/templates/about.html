About Umarket

Connecting Students at the University of Dodoma through a Seamless Online Marketplace.

Our Mission

At Umarket, we aim to empower the students of the University of Dodoma with a convenient platform for buying and selling goods and services within their community. Our mission is to foster entrepreneurship, promote accessibility, and ensure a safe and reliable marketplace experience.

Our Values

  • Integrity: Building trust within the UDOM community.
  • Innovation: Leveraging technology to simplify transactions.
  • Community: Strengthening student connections and collaborations.
  • Reliability: Ensuring safe and secure transactions.
